About EARTH

Project EARTH is an NFT collection that divides planet Earth into 812 nearly identically-spaced tiles.

  • Structure: The tile structure forms a truncated icosahedron where 12 pentagon tiles connect 20 patches of 40 hexagon tiles.
  • Ownable: Each tile is an NFT that can be acquired and traded at an NFT marketplace such as OpenSea.
  • Customizable: Tile owners can set a custom message for their tile and display it to the world.

View EARTH at OpenSea

The visualization is based on CesiumJS.
